About the SEA DRIF platform

Over the past decades, Southeast Asia has suffered from flooding on multiple occasions. In order to assist Lao PDR, Cambodia and Myanmar to increase their financial resilience against floods, the World Bank has commissioned the development of tools for support of rapid response financing in the wake of a natural disaster.
The project “South East Asia Flood Risk Assessment for Regional DRF Mechanism” has developed this platform to compute flood maps, population affected and return periods for floods in near-real time. The platform makes use of online gauge data, numerical model simulations and earth observation (EO) to make a best-estimate of the current flood impact.
